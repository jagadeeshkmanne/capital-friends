FAMILY MEMBERS

Edit Member:

[Click Family Members → Edit Member]

If you need to change anything later — phone number, email, address — click Edit Member. Select the member from dropdown. All fields are pre-filled. Change what you need, click Save.

View All:

This shows all family members with their details. Your family can open the app anytime and see everyone's details.

Edit / View All:

[Click Bank Accounts → Edit Account / View All]

INVESTMENT ACCOUNTS

[Click Investment Accounts → Add Account]

Investment accounts are wherever you invest — your broker, your MF platform, your demat account.

Enter Account Name, Family Member, Linked Bank Account (it shows our bank accounts!), Account Type, Platform, Client ID.

Click Add Account. Done!

[Add one or two more investment accounts quickly — for spouse, or a demat account]

Now your family knows exactly which banks and platforms you use, and who owns each account. If something happens to you, they know exactly where to look. That is the whole point of Capital Friends.

BEST PRACTICES

1. Add all family members — even if they don't invest. Your family needs to know who is who.

2. Add PAN and Aadhar — when you need to file taxes or claim insurance, these details save hours.

3. Add every bank account and investment account — even dormant ones. If something happens to you, your family should not have to guess.

4. Share access with your spouse right away — they should be able to see everything from day one.

5. Update whenever something changes — new bank account, new demat, closed account — keep it current.
