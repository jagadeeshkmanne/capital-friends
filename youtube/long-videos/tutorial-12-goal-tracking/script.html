BEST PRACTICES

1. Check the Goals Dashboard once a month — don't check daily. Long-term goals need patience.

2. Map one portfolio to one goal when possible — this gives the clearest picture. Split only if necessary.

3. If a goal shows "Behind" status — don't panic. Increase your SIP or add lumpsum rather than changing the goal target.

4. When a goal is near completion, shift to safer funds — move from equity to liquid/debt before withdrawing. Don't let a market crash destroy a goal that is 90% done.

5. Use Withdrawal Planning before redeeming — it helps you plan which fund to sell and shows tax implications.
