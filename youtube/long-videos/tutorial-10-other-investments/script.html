LIABILITIES (LOANS)

Many people only count assets — "I have X lakhs in investments!" But if you have loans, your real net worth is assets minus loans. Capital Friends shows the true picture.

We already added a Home Loan when we linked it to the property. Let me add another loan.

[In the sidebar, click Liabilities → Add Liability]

Select type (Home Loan, Auto Loan, Personal Loan, etc.). Enter lender, member, outstanding balance, EMI, interest rate, tenure. Click Add.

[In the sidebar, click Liabilities → View All]

All loans in one place with total liabilities.

In dashboard and email: Total Assets minus Total Liabilities = Real Net Worth. That is the number that matters.

When you pay off a loan — Edit Liability, change status to "Closed". Balance becomes zero. Your net worth goes up!

BEST PRACTICES

1. Update current values quarterly — gold price changes, FD matures, real estate appreciates. Keep numbers current.

2. Add all insurance policies — health, term, motor, home. Your family needs this information if something happens to you.

3. Always add nominee details to insurance — this is the most critical information for claims.

4. Link real estate to its loan — the dashboard then shows the net equity, not just the property value.

5. Update loan outstanding balance yearly — as you pay EMIs, the balance reduces and your net worth increases.

6. Focus on net worth, not just assets — ₹50 lakh in investments with ₹36 lakh in loans means only ₹14 lakh real net worth.
