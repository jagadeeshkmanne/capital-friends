REMINDERS

How many times have you missed an insurance renewal? Or forgot a tax filing deadline? Capital Friends sends you email alerts before any due date.

[In the sidebar, click Reminders → Add Reminder]

Insurance Renewal:

Select type Insurance Renewal — it auto-suggests a title and 60 days advance notice. Enter your policy name, due date, set frequency to Yearly, priority High. Click Add.

Monthly EMI:

[Add EMI reminder — Monthly frequency, set the date and advance notice days]

Tax Filing:

[Add Tax Filing reminder — Yearly, set due date and advance notice]

12 types available: Insurance, EMI, Tax Filing, SIP, Investment Review, Document Renewal, Fixed Deposit Maturity, and more.

Setup Daily Check:

[In the sidebar, click Reminders → Setup Daily Check]

This creates a trigger that runs at 8 AM every day. If any reminder is due within the advance notice period, you get an email. One click setup. Done!

Test Reminder Email:

[In the sidebar, click Reminders → Test Reminder Email]

Sends a test email right now to check if reminders are working.

Edit Reminder: Reminders → Edit Reminder — change due date, frequency, or delete.

View All: Reminders → View All Reminders — all reminders in one sheet.

FAMILY WEALTH EMAIL REPORT

This is the feature that makes Capital Friends truly special. Imagine every morning, your entire family gets a complete wealth report by email. Without opening the app.

Email Settings:

[Go to the Settings page in the app → Email Settings]

Enable Email Reports: Yes

Frequency: Daily (or Weekly/Monthly)

Time: 9 AM

Click Save. Every day at 9 AM, report is sent automatically. No manual work needed.

Who receives? All family members with "Include in Email Reports" = Yes. When you add family members in Family Members → Add Member, you check this box. Anyone who has it checked gets the email.

Send Now:

[Click Send Email Report → Yes]

[Show email in inbox]

Look at this email! Everything is here:

Summary: Total Net Worth, Total Assets, Total Liabilities

Mutual Funds: Each fund with NAV (Current/Avg), current value, P&L.

And below each fund — ATH info showing how far below the all-time high:

• 1-5% below ATH: goldenrod — small dip, normal

• 5-10% below: orange — worth watching

• 10-20% below: deep orange bold — significant correction

• 20%+ below: red bold — major dip, possible buying opportunity

The email also shows Unrealized P&L (blue — paper profit on holdings you still own) and Realized P&L (green — actual profit from withdrawals) separately. Your family sees the true picture.

Stocks: Live prices, P&L for each stock

Other Investments: Gold, PPF, Real Estate values

Insurance: All policies with sum assured and nominee

Goals: Progress for each financial goal

Action Items: From health check — what you need to fix (like "Create a Will")

Your family gets this every day. If something happens to you, they have everything — where the money is, how much, who is the nominee, which insurance to claim. That is the real purpose of Capital Friends.

DASHBOARD

[In the sidebar, click Dashboard]

Same information as the email — but viewable anytime inside the app.

Takes 10-30 seconds to generate because it reads all your sheets. But the result is a complete financial picture.
