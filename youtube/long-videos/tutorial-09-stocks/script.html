VIEW HOLDINGS — LIVE PRICES

[In the sidebar, click Stocks → View Holdings]

StockHoldings sheet shows all stocks with live current prices from Google Finance! The formula uses GOOGLEFINANCE() which updates automatically when the market is open. Current value, unrealized P&L — all live.

SELL STOCKS — FIFO P&L

[In the sidebar, click Stocks → Sell Stocks]

Select your portfolio and stock. It shows available quantity. Enter the number of shares to sell, sell price, and brokerage.

See the preview — it shows sale proceeds, cost basis (using FIFO — oldest shares first), brokerage, and your profit or loss. Everything calculated automatically.

[Point to the preview — explain: sold at higher price than buy price = profit. Brokerage is deducted from both sides]

Click Sell.

What is FIFO? First In First Out. If you bought a stock at two different prices — when you sell, Capital Friends sells the oldest shares first. This is how tax calculation works in India for stocks held over 1 year.
