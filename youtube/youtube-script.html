SLIDE 5 What Capital Friends Does
Show the web app live — walk through the pages and features

Now let me show you the actual app. When you sign in at capitalfriends.in with your Google account, this is what you see. A clean, modern dashboard with a sidebar that takes you to every section. It works on your phone, tablet, or computer. Let me walk you through everything.

First, the Initial Setup. When you sign in for the first time, the app automatically creates a private Google Sheet in your own Google Drive. This sheet is yours — it stores all your financial data. The app is just a beautiful dashboard to view and manage this data. You don't have to do anything. One sign-in and everything is set up.

Financial Health Check. This is a small questionnaire right inside the app. It asks you simple questions — do you have health insurance? Do you have term insurance? Do you have a will? Do you have emergency fund? Based on your answers, it gives you a score. It tells you what is missing. I will talk about these questions later in this video.

Dashboard. This is the home page. It shows your complete family wealth in one screen. Total wealth, total liabilities, net worth, how your money is divided between equity, debt, and gold. Market cap allocation — large cap, mid cap, small cap. Everything in one beautiful view.

Email Reports. This is the best part. The app sends a complete wealth report email to your entire family. The email has everything — total wealth, each person's investments, profit and loss, bank accounts, insurance, mutual fund holdings with current NAV, all-time high tracking. You can set it to send daily, weekly, or monthly automatically. Your family always knows the complete picture.

Email Settings. In the Settings page, you can set the time and how often the email goes. Daily at 9 AM? Weekly on Monday? Monthly on 1st? You choose.

Goals. This is a very powerful feature. You have a Goal Planner wizard that helps you plan for retirement, emergency fund, house purchase, child education, child marriage, car — any goal you want. It calculates the exact monthly SIP you need. Or if you have some lumpsum money right now, it tells you how much to invest as lumpsum and how much SIP for the remaining. You can also see the Goals page to track all your goals, how much progress you made. There is even a Withdrawal Planning feature — when you reach your goal, it tells you how to take the money out.

Family Members. This page is where you add everyone in your family. Your wife, parents, children, anyone who should know about your money. Their name, email, phone, PAN, Aadhar — everything in one place. If something happens to you, your family has all the details they need.

Bank Accounts. Add all your bank accounts. Savings, current, any bank. Your family will know which banks you use and what accounts you have.

Investment Accounts. Your Demat accounts, mutual fund platforms like Groww, Zerodha, Kuvera — add all of them here. So your family knows where your investments are.

Mutual Funds. This is a big one. You can create portfolios, set target allocation for each fund, buy via SIP, buy lumpsum, sell, switch between funds. The app tracks everything — current NAV, units, invested amount, current value, profit and loss. It even shows all-time high NAV so you know how far below the peak you are. And the best part — portfolio rebalancing. You set your target, like 60% equity 40% debt. If the market moves and your allocation changes, it tells you exactly how much to invest where to bring it back to your plan. It even shows rebalanced SIP and rebalanced lumpsum amounts. There is also Fund Composition Breakdown that shows you how much of your money is in large cap, mid cap, small cap.

Stocks. Create stock portfolios, buy stocks, sell stocks. It tracks your holdings, all transactions, profit and loss. Live prices come from Google Finance. The stock database has more than 5000 NSE stocks.

Other Investments. This is for everything else. Gold, real estate, PPF, EPF, NPS, fixed deposits, bonds, crypto, or any custom type you want. Add it here.

Liabilities. Track all your loans and debts. Home loan, car loan, personal loan, education loan, credit card, gold loan, business loan — everything. So you know your complete picture — assets minus liabilities equals your real net worth.

Insurance. Add all your insurance policies. Health insurance, term insurance, life insurance, motor insurance. Policy number, sum insured, premium, expiry date — everything tracked.

Reminders. This is very useful. Set reminders for insurance renewal, SIP payment, EMI due date, tax filing deadline, credit card bill, portfolio review, or any custom reminder. It sends you email alerts before the due date so you never miss anything.

So that is everything Capital Friends does. All in one free web app. No downloads, no subscription. Your data stays in your own Google Drive. Private and secure.

SLIDE 7 Do You Have Health Insurance?

Now let me ask you some important questions. Please think honestly.

Number one. Do you have health insurance?

One hospital visit today can cost 5 lakh, 10 lakh, or even 20 lakh rupees. A major surgery or cancer treatment can cost 30 to 50 lakh. Without health insurance, all the money you saved for years can disappear in just a few months.

And the most important thing — the younger you buy health insurance, the cheaper it is. If you wait till you are older or if you get some health problem, the same plan costs double or triple. Some companies might even reject you. So buy when you are young and healthy.

I will tell you about myself. I am 41 years old. I have health insurance for my full family. I pay less than 4 thousand rupees per month. My base cover is 1 crore and the plan gives 5 times coverage — that means up to 5 crore protection. All this for less than 4 thousand per month. If I had taken this same plan at age 30, it would have been even cheaper. And if I wait till 50, it will cost much more.

Think about it. Less than 4 thousand per month. And your family is protected up to 5 crore. Without this, one medical emergency can destroy everything you built in your entire life.

Please get health insurance if you don't have it. It is the first step of financial planning.

SLIDE 8 Do You Have Term Insurance?

Number two. Do you have term insurance?

If something happens to you, who will take care of your family? Your EMIs, your children's school fees, daily expenses — everything needs money.

Term insurance is very cheap. For a 1 crore cover, you pay only 10,000 to 15,000 rupees per year. That's about 1,000 rupees per month. Less than what you spend on eating out.

Without term insurance, your family's financial safety disappears when you are gone. Please get it today.

SLIDE 9 Do You Have Emergency Fund?

Number three. Do you have an emergency fund?

What if you lose your job? What if there is a medical emergency? What if something breaks in your house?

Without emergency funds, you will be forced to sell your investments at a loss. Keep at least 6 months of expenses in a savings account or liquid fund. If your monthly expenses are 50,000 rupees, keep 3 lakh rupees aside. This is your safety net.

Capital Friends has a Goal Planner that helps you calculate your exact emergency fund amount. Use it.

SLIDE 10 Does Your Family Know Where All Your Assets Are?

Number four. Does your family know where all your assets are?

You might have money in 5 different banks. Mutual funds in 3 different apps. Insurance policies in different companies. Property papers in different lockers.

If your family does not know where everything is, they will spend months searching. And some money they will never find. It will be lost forever.

This is exactly why Capital Friends exists. Put everything in one app. The email report goes to everyone automatically. Your family always knows.

SLIDE 11 Do You Have a Registered Will?

Number five. Do you have a registered will?

Without a will, your family will have to go to court to get your assets. This can take months or even years. And it costs a lot of money in lawyers and fees.

Making a will is simple and not expensive. Please do it. And use Capital Friends to track all your assets so your will is complete and nothing is missed.

SLIDE 12 Are Your Nominees Updated Everywhere?

Number six. Are your nominees updated everywhere?

Many people still have old nominees on their bank accounts and insurance. Maybe you recently got married. Maybe you have kids now. Maybe you changed your address. But your nominees are still the old ones.

Outdated nominees can delay your family's claims for months or years. Please check and update your nominees in every bank account, every insurance policy, every investment account. Do it today.

Use it. Set your goals today.
