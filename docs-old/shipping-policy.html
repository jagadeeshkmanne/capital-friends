No Physical Products

Capital Friends is a 100% digital product. It is a Google Sheets template that you copy directly to your Google Drive using Google's native copy feature.

There is no shipping because:

  • No physical goods are sold
  • No product is mailed or delivered
  • Everything happens instantly online

How You Receive Capital Friends

Instant Digital Delivery:

  1. Visit capitalfriends.in
  2. Click "Make a Copy"
  3. Google creates a copy in YOUR Google Drive instantly (takes 2 seconds)
  4. Start using immediately

No waiting. No delivery time. No shipping charges.
