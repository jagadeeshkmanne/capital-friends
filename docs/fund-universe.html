Why This Specific List of Funds?

No separate large, mid, small cap funds

Flexi cap and multi cap funds already spread your money across all market capitalizations. The fund manager rebalances between large, mid, and small caps based on market conditions. You don't need to manage 3 separate funds and worry about getting the ratio right. One flexi cap or multi cap fund does it all.

No single-index funds like Nifty 50 or Nifty Next 50

Instead of betting on one index, we include multi-factor and momentum-quality funds that give broader factor exposure. These combine momentum, quality, value, and low-volatility factors — less concentration risk than putting everything in one index. The Edelweiss Momentum Quality 50 and Mirae Multi Factor funds cover this better.

No pure debt funds

Multi asset and hybrid funds already carry 15-50% debt internally. ICICI Multi Asset has 20-30% debt. HDFC BAF dynamically adjusts debt. MO Multi Asset Conservative has 50% in gilt. Adding a separate debt fund on top creates unnecessary overlap and makes rebalancing harder. Exception: ICICI Equity Savings Fund for ultra-conservative investors who want debt-like stability with equity taxation benefits.

No pure gold funds (except one)

Multi asset funds already include gold (7-25%). ICICI Multi Asset has 10%+ in gold and silver. DSP Multi Asset can go up to 50% gold. But if you want a higher precious metals allocation, Mirae Gold & Silver ETF Fund of Fund gives you dedicated exposure with a flexible gold-silver split.

Multi asset funds are the core

A single multi asset fund gives you equity + debt + gold + sometimes international — all in one place with automatic rebalancing. This is the most efficient way to diversify. Pair one multi asset fund with a flexi cap or multi cap for alpha, and you have a complete portfolio in just 2-3 funds.

Active + Passive + Factor in each category

Each category includes different investment styles. Active funds have skilled managers picking stocks. Passive funds track indices at very low cost. Factor funds use rules-based strategies (momentum, quality etc.). Pick the style that matches your belief — or mix them for diversification.

How to Build Your Portfolio

A good portfolio is about getting the right asset allocation (equity vs debt vs gold vs international) and market cap allocation (large vs mid vs small cap). Pick 3 to 5 funds that together give you the allocation you want.
