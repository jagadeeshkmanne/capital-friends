Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Capital Friends ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free, open-source financial tracking tool built with Google Apps Script. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Web App Usage Tracking

When you click "Get Your Free Copy" on the web app, we collect:

  • Email Address: Your Google account email (obtained when you sign in)
  • Timestamp: Date and time when you clicked the button
  • Copy URL: The Google Sheets template link you were directed to

Storage: This data is logged in a secure admin Google Sheet (ID: 16QXJW6PSVP-NRcZV3UIw_xcRAT4bZD5IujzXMxjvy1M) accessible only to the developer.

Purpose: To count how many users are using Capital Friends and how many times each user creates copies (for support and analytics).

2.2 Spreadsheet Copy Data (Legacy - If Applicable)

For older versions that included questionnaires:

  • Security Questionnaire Answers: Your 10 Yes/No answers to financial security questions
  • Questionnaire Score: Your calculated financial readiness score (out of 10)

Note: The current web app version does NOT collect questionnaire data.

3. What Data We DO NOT Collect

We explicitly DO NOT collect, store, or access:

  • Your financial data (account balances, transactions, net worth, etc.)
  • Personal financial information entered in your spreadsheet
  • Contents of your Google Drive files
  • Spreadsheet IDs or tracking identifiers
  • Any data beyond the email notification mentioned in Section 2

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the minimal collected data ONLY for:

  • User Count Analytics: To understand how many people are using Capital Friends
  • Copy Frequency Tracking: To see how many times each user creates copies (helps identify issues)
  • Support & Troubleshooting: If you contact us for help, we can look up your usage history
  • Creating Your Spreadsheet Copy: Copy is created directly in YOUR Google Drive using your permissions

We do NOT: Sell your data, show ads, share with third parties, or use it for marketing.

5. Where Your Data is Stored

100% of your data stays in YOUR Google Drive. When you create a copy, the spreadsheet is created directly in your Drive and you are the owner from the start.

Your Financial Data Location: 100% in your personal Google Drive account

Developer Access: We have ZERO access to your spreadsheet after creation. The app runs with YOUR permissions only.

No Central Database: We do NOT maintain a user database or store any information centrally.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

The only data sharing that occurs:

  • Google Services: The app runs on Google's infrastructure (Apps Script, Drive, Sheets)
  • Admin Sheet Tracking: Your email and timestamp are logged in our admin Google Sheet when you click "Get Your Free Copy"
  • Developer Access Only: Only the developer (jagadeesh.k.manne@gmail.com) has access to the tracking sheet

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your Spreadsheet: Stored indefinitely in YOUR Google Drive until you delete it
  • Admin Sheet Tracking: Your email and timestamps are retained in the admin sheet for analytics and support
  • Deletion Requests: Contact us at jagadeesh.k.manne@gmail.com to request removal of your tracking data
  • Your Rights: You can delete your spreadsheet copy from your Drive anytime - we cannot delete it for you since it's in YOUR account

8. Google API Services User Data Policy

Capital Friends' use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

9. OAuth Scopes & Permissions

9.1 Web App Scopes (When You Click "Get Your Free Copy")

The web app requests minimal scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets: To access the template spreadsheet and redirect you to the copy URL
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email: To get your Google account email for tracking purposes

Deployment Mode: "Execute as: User deploying the web app" with access set to "Anyone"

9.2 Spreadsheet Copy Scopes (After You Create Your Copy)

Your spreadsheet copy will request these scopes when you first use features:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets: To manage your spreadsheet data, update NAVs, and generate reports
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send: To send automated weekly email reports from YOUR Gmail to recipients you configure
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.scriptapp: To run the custom menu system, triggers, and automation features

Important: These spreadsheet permissions apply ONLY to YOUR copy in YOUR Drive:

  • The spreadsheet runs with YOUR permissions only
  • YOU are the owner from the moment it's created
  • The developer has ZERO access to your spreadsheet or financial data
  • The app can ONLY access the specific spreadsheet you're using, not your other Drive files

10. Security Measures

  • App runs on Google's secure Apps Script infrastructure
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • App executes with YOUR permissions only (not developer's)
  • No central database means no central point of attack
  • No third-party analytics or tracking scripts

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Full Ownership: You own your spreadsheet 100% from creation
  • Control: Delete your spreadsheet copy from your Drive anytime
  • Revoke Access: Revoke app permissions in Google Account settings
  • Email Deletion: Request deletion of email notifications sent to developer
  • No Database Access: Since we don't maintain a database, there's no central data to access

12. Children's Privacy

Capital Friends is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

14. Open Source & Transparency

Capital Friends is open source. You can review the complete code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices.
