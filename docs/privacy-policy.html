Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Capital Friends ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free, open-source financial tracking tool built with Google Apps Script. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

2. What Data We Collect

When you use Capital Friends, we collect minimal data via email notification only:

  • Email Address: Your Google account email (sent to developer via email notification)
  • Security Questionnaire Answers: Your 10 Yes/No answers to financial security questions
  • Creation Timestamp: Date and time when you created your copy
  • Questionnaire Score: Your calculated financial readiness score (out of 10)

Important: We do NOT maintain a central database. Data is sent to the developer via email notification only.

3. What Data We DO NOT Collect

We explicitly DO NOT collect, store, or access:

  • Your financial data (account balances, transactions, net worth, etc.)
  • Personal financial information entered in your spreadsheet
  • Contents of your Google Drive files
  • Spreadsheet IDs or tracking identifiers
  • Any data beyond the email notification mentioned in Section 2

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the minimal collected data ONLY for:

  • Creating Your Spreadsheet Copy: Created directly in YOUR Google Drive using your account permissions
  • Developer Notifications: Email sent to developer when a new user creates a copy (for support purposes)
  • No Central Tracking: We do NOT track usage, store user lists, or maintain databases

5. Where Your Data is Stored

100% of your data stays in YOUR Google Drive. When you create a copy, the spreadsheet is created directly in your Drive and you are the owner from the start.

Your Financial Data Location: 100% in your personal Google Drive account

Developer Access: We have ZERO access to your spreadsheet after creation. The app runs with YOUR permissions only.

No Central Database: We do NOT maintain a user database or store any information centrally.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

The only data sharing that occurs:

  • Google Services: The app runs on Google's infrastructure (Apps Script, Drive, Sheets)
  • Email Notifications: When you create a copy, an email is sent to the developer with your email, score, and timestamp

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your Spreadsheet: Stored indefinitely in YOUR Google Drive until you delete it
  • Email Notifications: Developer may retain email notifications for support purposes
  • No Centralized Storage: We do not maintain a database, so there's no central data to delete
  • Deletion Requests: Contact us at jagadeesh.k.manne@gmail.com to request email deletion

8. Google API Services User Data Policy

Capital Friends' use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

9. OAuth Scopes & Permissions

Capital Friends requests these Google OAuth scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets: To create and set up your financial tracking spreadsheet
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file: To create the spreadsheet file in YOUR Google Drive
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send: To send weekly email reports from YOUR Gmail account
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.scriptapp: To run the web app and process your requests

Important: The app runs as "Execute as: User accessing the web app" which means:

  • The spreadsheet is created directly in YOUR Drive using YOUR permissions
  • YOU are the owner from the moment it's created
  • The developer has ZERO access to your spreadsheet or data
  • The app can ONLY access files it creates for you, not your other Drive files

10. Security Measures

  • App runs on Google's secure Apps Script infrastructure
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • App executes with YOUR permissions only (not developer's)
  • No central database means no central point of attack
  • No third-party analytics or tracking scripts

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Full Ownership: You own your spreadsheet 100% from creation
  • Control: Delete your spreadsheet copy from your Drive anytime
  • Revoke Access: Revoke app permissions in Google Account settings
  • Email Deletion: Request deletion of email notifications sent to developer
  • No Database Access: Since we don't maintain a database, there's no central data to access

12. Children's Privacy

Capital Friends is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

14. Open Source & Transparency

Capital Friends is open source. You can review the complete code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices.

15. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, data requests, or questions:

  • Email: jagadeeshi.k.manne@gmail.com
  • GitHub Issues: Report an issue
