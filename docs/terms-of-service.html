2. Description of Service

Capital Friends is a free, open-source Google Sheets template designed to help families track their wealth, manage financial information, and maintain financial security practices.

2.1 How It Works

  1. You Request a Copy: You visit our web app and click "Get My Copy"
  2. We Create Your Copy: Our system creates a copy of the master template stored in our Google Drive
