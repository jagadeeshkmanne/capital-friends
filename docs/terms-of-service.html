8. Permissions Required After Copying

After you create your copy, the spreadsheet will ask for permissions when you first use features:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send - To send automated weekly email reports from YOUR Gmail to recipients you configure
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.scriptapp - To run the custom menu system, triggers, and automation features

Important: These permissions apply ONLY to YOUR copy in YOUR Drive. They do NOT give us access to your data.
