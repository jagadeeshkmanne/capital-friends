Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Capital Friends

Is my financial data really private?

▼
100% YES. Your data never leaves your Google Drive. The sheet is created directly in YOUR Google Drive account, and we have ZERO access to it. We don't store, view, or collect any of your financial information. It's completely private - just like any other Google Sheet you own.

How do the rebalancing alerts work?

▼
You set your target allocation (e.g., 70% equity, 30% debt). The sheet automatically tracks your portfolio daily. When your portfolio drifts beyond your threshold (e.g., becomes 75% equity, 25% debt), you get an email alert with smart suggestions like: "Invest ₹50,000 in debt funds to rebalance back to 70-30." No more daily checking - just act when needed!

Do I need to manually update prices/NAVs?

▼
Mutual Funds: NAVs update automatically daily using Google Finance formulas.
Stocks: Coming Soon! Individual stock price tracking is under development and will be added in the next update.
Other investments (FDs, gold, real estate, PPF): You update manually when values change. This ensures you always know the current state of your portfolio.

How does my family access everything in case of emergency?

▼
This is the PRIMARY reason Capital Friends exists! The sheet lives in YOUR Google Drive. Here's how your family stays informed:
  • Share the sheet with spouse/family members (View or Edit access)
  • They receive automatic daily/weekly email reports - always up to date
  • In an emergency, they simply open the sheet - no passwords needed
  • They see EVERYTHING: what you own, what you owe, where it all is
  • All account numbers, broker details, insurance policies fully documented
Complete peace of mind - your family is never left in the dark.

Is this really free? What's the catch?

▼
100% free. Forever. No catch. This is an open-source project built to help families manage wealth better. We don't:
  • ❌ Charge subscription fees
  • ❌ Show ads
  • ❌ Sell your data
  • ❌ Require credit cards
  • ❌ Have premium tiers
Everything is in YOUR Google Drive. You own it completely. We simply provide the template.

Does this integrate with my bank/broker?

▼
No, and that's by design. Bank integrations require you to share login credentials and give apps access to your accounts. We believe that's risky. Instead:
  • You manually enter ALL your investments (one-time setup)
  • Only mutual fund NAVs update automatically - stocks & other assets are manual
  • You update balances when needed (weekly/monthly)
Yes, it takes a bit more effort initially. But you get complete privacy and security - no third-party apps accessing your bank accounts.

Can I track multiple family members?

▼
Yes! That's a core feature. You can add:
  • Your spouse's investments and accounts
  • Parents' portfolios
  • Children's accounts
  • Joint holdings
The dashboard shows total family net worth AND individual breakdowns. Perfect for getting a complete family financial picture.

I'm not good with spreadsheets. Can I still use this?

▼
Absolutely! The sheet is pre-built with:
  • Clear instructions in each tab
  • Simple data entry forms (just fill in the blanks)
  • Automatic calculations - you never touch formulas
  • Beautiful dashboards that update automatically
If you can use Google Sheets at a basic level (type and click), you can use this. No technical knowledge required.

How do I get email alerts? Do I need to set up anything?

▼
The sheet has built-in Google Apps Script that can send emails. First time you enable alerts:
  • Click "Enable Email Alerts" button in the sheet
  • Google will ask for permission (one-time)
  • Set your preferences (weekly reports, rebalancing alerts, etc.)
  • Done! Emails will come from your own Google account
Takes 2 minutes to set up. After that, it's fully automated.

Can I customize the sheet for my needs?

▼
100% YES! Once the sheet is in your Drive, you own it completely. You can:
  • Add/remove columns and sections
  • Change colors and formatting
  • Add your own investment categories
  • Modify formulas (if you're comfortable)
  • Share with anyone you want
It's YOUR sheet. Do whatever works best for your family.
