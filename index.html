Capital Friends

One dashboard for your family's entire wealth

Capital Friends is a free, open-source family portfolio tracker built by Jagadeesh Manne. Track mutual funds, stocks, FDs, gold, insurance, loans, and goals for your entire family — all stored privately in your Google Drive. No servers, no database, no tracking.

What makes Capital Friends different

  • ATH Tracking — See how far each mutual fund is from its All-Time High NAV. Spot buying opportunities when quality funds dip below ATH.
  • Smart Rebalancing — Set target allocation for each fund. Get exact buy/sell/SIP adjustment amounts when your portfolio drifts.
  • Goal-Based Investing — Link portfolios to goals like retirement, education, or emergency fund. Track progress with on-track indicators.
  • Family Dashboard — Track wealth for your entire family. Switch between members, see combined or individual net worth.
  • Your Data, Your Drive — Everything stored in a Google Spreadsheet in your own Google Drive. No external servers, no database.
  • Email Reports & Reminders — Scheduled portfolio summaries sent from your Gmail. SIP reminders, insurance renewals, goal check-ins.

How it works

  1. Sign in with your Google account — no registration, no passwords
  2. Capital Friends creates a private spreadsheet in your Google Drive
  3. Start tracking — add family members, portfolios, goals, insurance, loans

No bank credentials needed — manual entry means you control what goes in. Always free, no ads, no premium plans. Open source on GitHub.
